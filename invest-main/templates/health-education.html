Suncrest College —
Yorkton's Post-Secondary Hub

Founded in 2023 through the merger of Cumberland College and Parkland College, Suncrest College is now one of Saskatchewan's most innovative regional post-secondary institutions — and it calls Yorkton home.

★ Yorkton Campus Highlight

Skills. Research. Community.

Suncrest College delivers university degrees in partnership with the University of Saskatchewan and University of Regina, alongside trades training, health sciences, and one-of-a-kind programs you can't find anywhere else in the province. With 74 active applied research projects and international partnerships spanning Jamaica, Kenya, Tanzania, Belize, and South Africa — this is not a small regional college. It's a genuine institution.

Programs at Suncrest are designed around real regional workforce needs. Every graduate enters a market that is actively recruiting — businesses in the area provide practicum placements and hire Suncrest graduates directly.

Services Available in Yorkton

  • 24-Hour Emergency Department with full triage
  • General Surgery, Obstetrics & Gynecology (4 OR suites)
  • Maternity care — low to moderate risk pregnancies, C-sections, epidurals
  • Pediatric inpatient unit (children 18 and under)
  • Diagnostic Imaging — including new Sonography training program
  • Stroke Rehabilitation (Jowsey House inpatient program)
  • Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy & Speech Language Pathology
  • Mental Health & Addictions inpatient services
  • Palliative & supportive care — cancer, cardiovascular, neurological
