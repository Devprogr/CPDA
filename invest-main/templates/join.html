Who We Are

The Central Prairie Development Alliance (CPDA) is a regional initiative uniting communities across east-central Saskatchewan and west-central Manitoba. We believe shared challenges are best solved together.

Why Become a Member?

1) Grant Writing Support & Review

Increase your chances of securing vital provincial and federal funding.

2) Economic Development Expertise

Collaborate on regional planning, workforce development, and investment attraction.

3) Joint Infrastructure & Purchasing Opportunities

Participate in regional RFPs and access savings through bulk/cooperative purchasing.

4) Regional Advocacy & Visibility

Strength in numbers when lobbying for healthcare, transportation, housing, and more.

5) Networking & Shared Knowledge

Learn from and work alongside other municipalities, First Nations, and local organizations.
