Who Can Join?

CPDA welcomes everyone who shares a stake in the future of East-Central Saskatchewan. Whether you lead, live, work, or represent — there is a place for you here.

The Case for Joining

Why Join?

Influence the region's future and help shape a vibrant, inclusive community for generations to come.

What You Gain

A Voice That Carries Further

As part of a regional alliance, your perspective reaches provincial and federal decision-makers with far greater weight than any single community speaking alone.

Regional Connections

Connect with leaders, businesses, and community builders across 16 member communities — building relationships that open doors and create shared opportunities.

Shape the Regional Agenda

Participate in sub-committees and working groups that define CPDA's priorities — from housing and transport to economic development and cultural investment.

Build for Generations

Help shape a vibrant, inclusive community for generations to come — contributing to long-term prosperity and quality of life across East-Central Saskatchewan.
